AMRITA TEC provides the innovator the infrastructure for Technology Development to create a proof of concept of the Technology. For the innovator to iterate their idea, we provide rapid prototyping, facility through digital fabrication. The facility includes wide range of Digital Fabrication tools such as,

3D printer

CNC Machine

PCB Printers
